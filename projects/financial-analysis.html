Key analytical findings

  • Platform commission costs (delivery apps) were the largest margin drag — renegotiation and direct channel promotion drove 4pp improvement
  • Product mix shift toward higher-margin items contributed 3pp — identified through SKU-level profitability analysis
  • Labour cost optimisation via scheduling model contributed 3pp across all three locations
  • Dynamic pricing on peak periods (weekends, lunch) contributed a further 3pp
